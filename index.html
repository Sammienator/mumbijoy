A little bit more About Mumbi Joy

Sometimes it’s easy to lose sight of the why of what we do. Maybe we started off with inspiration and lost it somewhere along the way. Because other things came up. Because life demanded it. Because money was offered. Because we wanted to promote a certain appearance. This happened to me in my journey to learn web development and I feel that it’s important to reconnect with why I do this and why I started.I started developing websites five months ago.
